THE WITHERING

Four Clans–Ridgeclan, Brackenclan, Mistclan and Breezeclan–have long lived in quiet balance across their forest, plains, wetland, and mountain homes. But now, something foul rides the wind. A stench of disease, decay, and death seeps through the trees, clings to the mist, and settles in the bones of the land. Peace is crumbling, and no cat can imagine what darkness has begun to take root.
