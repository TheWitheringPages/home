The Withering

It does not kill fast. It changes. It begins with a cough, a fever, a thirst that water cannot quench. The eyes change first. Then the hunger. Not for prey, but for something else entirely. The connection to StarClan is severed. In its place, a new voice whispers. It promises strength. It promises an end to suffering.

The afflicted are faster, stronger, at least for a period. Their pelts become dull, their scent wrong. They no longer feel pain, or fear, or loyalty. They are merely vessels for the entities. The only prevention is the pink sauce, a concoction from a fallen entity.
